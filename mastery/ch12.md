## Pitfalls Along the Path

The thirteen pitfalls:

 1. conflicting way of life
 2. obsessive goal orientation
 3. poor instruction
 4. lack of competitiveness
 5. overcompetitiveness
 6. laziness
 7. injuries
 8. drugs
 9. prizes and medals
 10. vanity
 11. dead seriousness
 12. inconsistency
 13. perfectionism
